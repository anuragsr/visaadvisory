What to observe in case of illness

When you sign up for the VisaAdvisory Student health insurance your insurance documents include a a treatment certificate. You must present the treatment slip to the physician or hospital prior to the treatment in Germany. You do not need the treatment slip outside Germany. In case of acute illness or accident you can choose your physician or hospital. For general illnesses such as influenza, headaches or minor accidents we recommend you visit a general practitioner. Your attending physician can refer you to a specialist, if required. In case of toothache, contact a dentist directly. In case of severe pain or major accidents outside of regular physician`s hours (e.g. at the weekend) please head directly to a hospital. Please contact us directly should you require an inpatient treatment at the hospital . The staff of the emergency hotline will coordinate the assumption of the costs with the hospital directly. This offers you the advantage of not having to first pay high hospital costs yourself as the insurer pays the hospital directly. You can reach our emergency hotline staff around the clock: Telephone: +49 89 624 21 – 46

Reimbursement of invoices

Your physician or hospital will present you with an invoice for visits without overnight stays (out-patient treatment). You must transfer the payment to the attending physician or the hospital. Cash payment is only requested in rare cases. In order for you to receive quick reimbursement from the insurance company, you need to supply us with the following information: Your name Your insurance number Treatment data from the physician (incl. diagnosis) Medical services + overview of costs If applicable, prescriptions (with medication, price and stamp of the pharmacy) prescribed the physician You can conveniently send us the information via e-mail or post. The processing usually takes 2 weeks.

Limitations of the health insurance

§ 4 What restrictions of the insurance cover must be observed? No insurance cover exists for Medical treatment and other medically prescribed measures which are an occasion for the stay in the agreed area of application; Medical treatment and other medically prescribed measures, the necessity of which was known to the insured person prior to the stay in the agreed area of application or at the time of the conclusion of the insurance or which he had to expect according to the circumstances known to him; Food and tonic; orthodontic treatments, dental treatments that go beyond pain-relieving treatments, repairs of dental prostheses and temporaries;.

General information about the German health insurance system

As a welfare state, Germany aims to have comprehensive primary health care for the entire population. One of the pillars of this philosophy is the German healthcare system. This includes both statutory and private health insurance. Different groups of persons in Germany are subject to compulsory health insurance. This includes all students enrolled in state-run or state-recognized institutions of higher education under the age of 30. At the beginning of their studies, students are allocated to a statutory health insurance company. From the time of enrolment students have three months to decide whether they want to be insured via statutory health insurance (GKV) or a private health insurance (PKV). This decision is binding and cannot be reversed for the duration of the enrolment.

Can I also obtain insurance from you if I have been in Germany for a while?

Yes, you can obtain insurance from us if you are enrolled in university in Germany and are not residing longer than five years in Germany or if the entire insurance duration is no longer than five years..

What should I select when I do not exactly know yet how long I will be staying in Germany?

We recommend that you apply for the maximum insurance duration as a subsequent application for extension may be rejected. With a longer duration you will also continue to have health coverage if you stay longer than planned in Germany. If you decide to leave early you can cancel your insurance contract at any time without stating reasons to the end of each month (by a short e-mail).
